Supporting Grassroots Innovation Internationally

Our evaluation process assesses regional needs instead of limiting evaluations to individual country constraints. Whether you design off-grid learning solutions in the Caribbean Islands, solar water filtration in South Asia, or winter greenhouse insulation in Eastern Europe, we analyze viability based on resources available directly in your area.

We prioritize solutions engineered from raw materials easily accessible to local workshops, family cooperatives, or community groups. No expensive or high-tech equipment is required, simply an honest and viable 2-minute pitch detailing your project setup.

Standard 2-Minute Pitch Guidelines 120 Seconds Max

1
Introduction and Region (0s to 30s)

Introduce your name, team context, and your targeted region.

2
Challenge and Solution (30s to 90s)

Detail what community equipment or micro-enterprise setup you will establish.

3
Grant Deployment (90s to 120s)

Explain how your selected tier amount (e.g. $1,000) launches this initial pilot.

No complex application form required.
